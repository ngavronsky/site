[Welcome](https://heywelcome.com) makes it easy to benchmark and communicate compensation to candidates and employees, powered by our real-time compensation data. 

Welcome was [acquired by BambooHR](https://medium.com/welcomehr/welcome-has-been-acquired-by-bamboohr-9ada34fb7659) in 2022, where our team is continuing our mission to reimagine compensation.
